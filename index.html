Sim-Sala-Sheep

The town of Ovisbury has fallen out of favor with their god! Once every two days, the lowest-ranking member of the society will be sacrificed unless the concerning issue is solved. Help the society or save yourself, the choice is yours. Good luck!

Game

The town of Ovisbury is in disorder. Every other day, the town faces a new crisis. The townspeople solve all their problems with rituals: it's your job to avoid becoming the sacrifice!

Ovisbury is old-fashioned; everything revolves around their social hierarchy. The lowest ranked person gets sacrificed, unless their problems are resolved in time! Help out the villagers and solve the problems, or sabotage them for personal gain.

Hints

  • The villagers have a daily schedule. Figure out when they aren't home so you can steal their belongings.
  • Learn their likes and dislikes; bring items they like to raise your rank or bring items they hate to lower theirs.
  • Bring the right items to the bottom of the great tree to solve the ongoing crisis.
  • Advance the time by moving through a door.
  • Pen and paper are your friends! Take lots of notes.

Controls

Left: Move left
Right: Move right
Up: Enter doors
Space: Pick up or throw items
